About SolveNow

How we build things

We started SolveNow because we kept seeing real, everyday problems that technology should have solved by now — but hasn't.

1

Start with a real problem

Not a technology, not a trend — a genuine pain point we've experienced ourselves. A family scrambling after a crisis. A business losing thousands to expired licenses.

2

Build the simplest solution

No bloat, no unnecessary features. We build the minimum viable tool that actually solves the problem. If it needs three buttons instead of thirty, that's what we ship.

3

Make it accessible

Free tiers, mobile-first, works on any device. We believe essential tools shouldn't be locked behind expensive subscriptions or complex setups.
