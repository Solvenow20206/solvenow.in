Real Stories

This happens every day

These aren't rare edge cases. They're the everyday reality for millions of Indian families.

💔

The FD Nobody Knew About

A father passes away. Months later, the family discovers he had 3 fixed deposits across different banks — but no nominee was updated. The maturity dates pass. The money moves to RBI's unclaimed pool. The family never knows.

📋

The Insurance That Lapsed

A husband had a ₹50 lakh term insurance policy. After his death, his wife didn't know it existed. The premium auto-debit stopped. The policy lapsed. IRDAI data shows 90% of unclaimed insurance is from agent-sold policies where families have no records.

⚖️

The Inheritance Dispute

Two brothers, a family property, no documented will. One claims their father promised him the house. The case drags on for 8 years in court. 40% of all will-related cases in India involve validity challenges.

🏦

The Scattered Investments

A working professional has mutual funds with 3 AMCs, stocks in 2 demat accounts, PPF, NPS, and crypto. After a sudden health emergency, his family can't locate half of it. Each platform needs separate claims, separate paperwork.

Getting Started

How it works

Three steps to protect your family's financial future.

1

Set Up Your Drawers

Create Drawers for each group — Family, Personal, CA, Business Partner. Inside each Drawer, your documents are sorted into Folders: Insurance, Health, Property, Bank, and 100+ more.

2

Add Trusted People

Invite your spouse, children, or trusted advisor. Assign them to the Drawer they should access. They get their documents on web, mobile, or WhatsApp — no extra setup for them.

3

Set Up Your Safety Net

Configure the inactivity timer. If you don't check in, Virasat automatically grants access to your nominees. You stay in control.
